Investment Banking Calculator

By: Aditya Aysola

Enter the initial amount (P-value)

Enter the number of times compounded

Enter the rate

Enter the time in years



The formula is: A=P(1+r/n)^(nt)

P = principle amount (investment)
r = Rate (convert percent to decimal)
n = Number of times compounded
t = Time in years
A = Future Value
